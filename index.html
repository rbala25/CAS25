Intro

It is clear to all that climate change is the biggest issue in the 21st century. As more research is being done, the issue of climate change is getting more and more severe. With our habits right now, it is predicted that we will reach a tipping point in the early 2030s. With this date being so soon it is extremely important to severely slow down and hopefully put an end to climate change.

Statistics show that the majority of children and teens are very afraid of the effects of climate change, however, it is not very easy for them to take initiative. Our goal as a non-profit organization is to unite the young generation through education to provide easy and effective methods for Earth’s inheritors to take action on climate change. While progress is being made, it is nowhere near enough. The majority of people taking initiative currently are people who will hardly experience the extreme effects of climate change. As climate change is a direct threat to the younger generations, it is essential that we form a strong community to prevent climate change.

Work

Our first project for this year is called “This is the planet we will inherit”. Our goal is to reach as many schools as possible in New Jersey, especially underprivileged communities, and make a small lesson plan about climate change. Our goal from phase 1 of this project is to get kids from elementary school and middle school thinking about climate change.

Phase 2 involves a large banner. This banner will have the text “This is the planet we will inherit”. On this banner we plan to have the artwork (could be poems, drawings, mini-essays) of these children that we thought. This artwork will be centered around their opinions and statements about climate change and the way it affects them. Students could write about the fear of climate change, or make drawings of how the world will look once these feedback loops take place. In general, phase 2 of this project is all about taking the ideas of the next generation and putting them on this banner.

Once this is complete, phase 3 involves hanging this banner around North New Jersey (in front of schools, buildings, ect) in order to make a statement about the next generation’s feelings about climate change. Our goal is to gather as much retention as possible in order to expand and strengthen our community. Not only do we hope to gain more supporters from younger kids like ourselves but we also plan to gain support from the adults in our community or organizations that can help us organize more projects.

While the idea is simple, we believe that this sense of community that we are forming is vital to saving our planet. As of now, the climate change prevention community is very small and while some young kids do help out there aren’t enough. As a non-profit and young adults ourselves we know that our peers want to help out and we believe that with unification, we can yield great results.

About

We are three 11th grade students from Newark Academy in Livingston, New Jersey. Our mission is to educate the younger generation about climate change. The goal of all the activities we do is to teach kids about sustainability and make sure they understand the importance of saving our planet and how to live sustainability. We have noticed that many kids want to help fight climate change, but don’t have this opportunity or sometimes even the education. We want to make it easy to make a difference in climate change. Our work aims to provide an easy way to educate children and provide them with safe and easy methods of fighting climate change.
